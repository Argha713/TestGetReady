What is gap analysis in education?

Gap analysis in education refers to the process of identifying discrepancies between current and desired educational performance.

Why is gap analysis important for educational institutions?

Gap analysis helps institutions pinpoint areas of improvement, ensuring effective strategies for educational enhancement.

How can schools benefit from conducting a gap analysis?

Schools can use gap analysis to identify curriculum gaps, improve teaching methodologies, and enhance student performance.

What steps are involved in conducting a gap analysis for education?

Steps include defining goals, assessing current performance, identifying gaps, formulating strategies, and implementing improvements.

How does gap analysis contribute to educational planning?

Gap analysis informs educational planning by identifying areas where improvements are needed, guiding resource allocation effectively.
